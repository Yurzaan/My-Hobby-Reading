I am Yurzaan Doctor and I like to read

My Hobby:Reading

My favourite hobby is reading. I enjoy reading a book when I am free. I started to do it when I was about four years old. The first time I did it, I felt interested. So I kept reading. The teachers always taught me to read the difficult words. I was happy when I read a story with a happy ending. I was thrilled when I read a detective story.

There are a lot of advantages of reading. Reading makes me feel relaxed and calm. I can also learn new vocabulary items. Then I can further improve my English. Moreover, it can give me an unlimited imagination, so I can write books in the future. I can learn about the different cultures and customs of other countries in the world too.
